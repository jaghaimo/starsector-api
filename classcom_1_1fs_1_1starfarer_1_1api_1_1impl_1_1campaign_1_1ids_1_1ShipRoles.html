◆ COMBAT_SMALL_FOR_SMALL_FLEET

final String com.fs.starfarer.api.impl.campaign.ids.ShipRoles.COMBAT_SMALL_FOR_SMALL_FLEET = "combatSmallForSmallFleet"
static

Excludes ships that are annoying to fight in low numbers - Omen, Hyperion, long-range beam/kiting loadouts, etc. Anything that's extremely likely to only end after full CR drain.

Phase ships are in their own roles so not included here either (and there's other logic to not add them to small fleets).

Also should not include escort/support type variants.
