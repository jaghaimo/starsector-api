Detailed Description

Variables set in memory that indicate certain decisions or behaviors of the AI. Used to communicate between otherwise-disconnected modules, such as ability AI and other fleet AI modules.

Mods replacing parts of the fleet AI should try to use these if possible/appropriate.

Mods replacing fleet AI entirely (i.e. all modules, and all ability AI) are free to either use these or devise their own system.
