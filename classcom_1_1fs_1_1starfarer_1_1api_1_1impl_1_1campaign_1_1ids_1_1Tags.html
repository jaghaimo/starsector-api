static

Stuff that shouldn't be able to be destroyed. Currently only works for campaign objectives. See also: MemFlags.STORY_CRITIAL, for things that are story critical with specific "reasons" and stop being critical once the reasons are all no longer valid.

◆ THEME_INTERESTING

final String com.fs.starfarer.api.impl.campaign.ids.Tags.THEME_INTERESTING = "theme_interesting"
static

Used to mark systems that would be "interesting" for the player to visit, due to ruins, derelicts, anything special, and so on. These systems will be more likely to be picked for containing the targets of randomly generated missions. Should ONLY be used where it's acceptable that this may happen.
